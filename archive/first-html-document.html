Some of Emily's Favorite Things

On this web page, I will be discussing my favorite place as well as some of my favorite types of food.

My Favorite Place

My favorite place is New Brighton Park in Vancouver, British Columbia. It is a short walk from my home - all you have to do is head straight down Wall Street to the overpass - then you can either continue on and enter the park by passing through a short tunnel and then walking by the pool, or you can climb the stairs to the overpass and use the bridge to enter near the dog park. It is a beautiful park with nice walking paths and an amazing view.

3 of my favorite things about New Brighton Park:

  • The outdoor swimming pool
  • The beach, with good logs for sitting and rocks for throwing
  • The view of the mountains, the Second Narrows Bridge, the grain terminal, and the water
