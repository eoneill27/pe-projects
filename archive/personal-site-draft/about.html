It's nice to meet you!

I am an American expat living in Vancouver, British Columbia. I moved here in 2005 to get my degree in Library Science and Archival Studies at the University of British Columbia, and I've been here ever since.

After 15 years of working as a librarian, I decided I needed a change. This led me to Perpetual Education and their course, Design for the Web. I'm moving through the course with the goal of branching out on my own and making a new career of helping people build beautiful, goal-oriented, user-friendly websites.

My time in librarianship has given me a good mind for information organization, which I bring to my web design work in the form of simple navigation and clear information structure. I want the information on your website to be easy for you and your customers to find. If this goal seems like a no-brainer, think of all the websites you've encountered where the opposite is true!

I look forward to hearing from you - I bet we'll work well together!
